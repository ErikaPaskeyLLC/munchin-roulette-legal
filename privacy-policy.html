Privacy Policy – Munchin’ Roulette

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

Erika Paskey LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information our mobile app Munchin’ Roulette collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data (ZIP/GPS): Used only to find restaurants through the Yelp API. We do not store this data.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We collect anonymous statistics about how the app is used (e.g., which screens are viewed, how often features are used) to improve app performance and design.
  • Crash & Performance Data: If the app crashes or errors, diagnostic information is sent automatically (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help us identify and fix problems.
  • Purchase & Trial Data: We securely track whether you are in a free trial or have purchased Pro features. All financial transactions are processed by the App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store payment card information.

2. How We Collect Information

We use trusted third-party services, including Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, which may automatically collect device type, operating system version, app usage patterns, and crash reports. This information is not personally identifiable.

3. How We Use Information

  • To monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand which features are most useful to users
  • To determine trial eligibility and manage Pro features
  • To improve the overall user experience

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. Anonymous analytics and crash data are processed by Firebase in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics and crash logs may be retained by Firebase for a limited period per their policies. We do not store your personal information on our servers.

6. Your Choices

  • You may disable analytics collection in your device’s privacy settings if supported by your platform.
  • You may delete the app at any time to stop all data collection.
